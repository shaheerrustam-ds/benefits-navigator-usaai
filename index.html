ℹ️ About & Privacy

🔒

Your data stays in your browser

No information you enter is sent to any server. Everything — your eligibility check history, language preference, and theme — is saved locally in your browser's localStorage only.

🚫

No tracking or analytics

This app does not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party tracking. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

🔑

Optional API key

If you choose to add a Gemini API key, it is stored only in your browser's localStorage and used exclusively for direct browser-to-Gemini API calls. It is never transmitted to any intermediate server.

🗑️

Clear your data any time

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or site data in your browser settings.
